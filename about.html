About me

Ziyi Jiang 1999

Hi, I am Ziyi Jiang (she/her/hers), a MSI 1st year student with UX track of University of Michigan. Previously, I was a Product Design and Manufacture student at University of Nottingham.

With my background in Human-Computer Interaction, I strive to design for the users of my product with empathy. I love to solve problems that are deeply rooted in the users with clear goals through research findings, quantitative data and my intuition as a passionate designer. I am interested in solving difficult problems, envisioning the role of new technologies in the future of people's lives and finding new solutions to design inclusive and accessible products & experiences.
